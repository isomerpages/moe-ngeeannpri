<p><strong>SYF Arts Presentation 2026</strong>
</p>
<p>The Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) continues to be a key platform organized
by the Arts Education Branch (AEB) under the Ministry of Education, celebrating
the artistic talents and dedication of students across Singapore. Through
the SYF Arts Presentation (AP), schools are given opportunities to showcase
their learning and growth in the arts while receiving valuable feedback
from professional adjudicators.</p>
<p>We are proud to share that our students demonstrated great resilience,
commitment, and passion throughout their months of preparation, culminating
in inspiring performances at the SYF Arts Presentation 2026. Our Malay
Dance and Guzheng groups were awarded Certificates of Accomplishment, while
our Chinese Orchestra, Choir, and Chinese Dance groups attained Certificates
of Distinction.</p>
<p>These achievements reflect not only the hard work and perseverance of
our students, but also the dedication and guidance of our teachers and
instructors who journeyed alongside them every step of the way.</p>
<p>Congratulations and thank you to everyone who made this meaningful experience
possible!
