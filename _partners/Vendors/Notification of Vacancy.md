<p><strong>Application Procedure</strong>
</p>
<p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Download and complete the application form from MOE
website. <a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.moe.gov.sg/canteens</a>.
You may also request for a hardcopy from the School’s General Office.
<br>
</p>
<p>2. Attach the following documents together with the application form:
<br>a. Photocopy of the NRIC (front and back)
<br>b. List of proposed menu and pricing for all items
<br>c. Photocopy of relevant certificates such as Food Safety Course Level
1, etc</p>
<p>3. Submit the completed application form and documents by closing date
via email OR to the School's General Office.</p>
<p>Address:&nbsp; 344 Marine Terrace S449149
<br>Contact No: 6441 8677
<br>Email: <a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">naps@moe.edu.sg</a>
<br>Contact Person: Mdm Mariam</p>
<p><strong>How to get here?</strong>
<br>Nearest MRT: Eunos MRT
<br>Bus number: 13, 16, 16M, 31, 36, 43, 47, 48, 55, 134, 150, 196, 197</p>
<p><strong>Interest applicants must meet the following criteria: </strong>
<br>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Singapore citizen or Singapore Permanent
Resident (SPR) for stallholder and stall assistant.
<br>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Able to provide good service and quality
food with prices determined in consultation with the school.
<br>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Able to provide cooked food that meets
the health / nutritional standards set by the Health Promotion Board (HPB)
under the "Healthy Meals in Schools Programme (HMSP)" guidelines at <a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.hpb.gov.sg/schools/school-programmes/healthy-meals-in-schools-programme</a>.
<br>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Able to maintain high standard of food
safety and personal hygiene.
<br>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Healthy and fit to operate the stall.</p>
<p><strong>Applicants should preferably fulfil the following criteria: </strong>
<br>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Possess catering experience or experience
operating in a school environment.
<br>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Successful applicant is required to apply
for a food stall license. They may apply for the license from the&nbsp;
<a href="https://licence1.business.gov.sg/licence1/neweadvisor/showSelectedLicence.action?redirection=true&amp;selectedLicenceIds=1901021401000441" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">GoBusiness website</a>.</p>
<p>Remarks: Shortlisted applicants will be notified for an interview and
will be required to provide food sampling for the School Canteen Selection
Committee at the applicant's own expense.</p>
